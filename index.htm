Why Azimuth?

New Eyes’ founder, Julia Lawrence Terry, is the inspiration for our name, Azimuth, a direction measured from the North used in navigation and mapping. Julia, pictured navigating her family at sea, charted our mission’s course in 1932 by providing new eyeglasses to financially struggling individuals in the U.S.

We all have our unique True North, the motivation that positively guides us as we travel along life’s path to our true self. At Azimuth & New Eyes, our True North is transforming lives through clear vision. With every purchase, New Eyes provides two individuals in financial need with new prescription eyeglasses, supporting your authentic inner self, your values, beliefs, and purpose. We will send you the first name, state and age of the two individuals who received new eyeglasses thanks to you!
