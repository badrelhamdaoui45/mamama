Join New Eyes on GivingTuesday 2020!

December 1, 2020 New Eyes

On Giving Tuesday, help New Eyes bring $2,500 worth of eyeglasses to kids and adults in need.

Giving Tuesday was created in 2012 as a simple idea: a day that encourages people to do good. Over the past nine years, this idea has grown into a global movement that inspires hundreds of millions of people to give, collaborate, and celebrate giving.  

By joining New Eyes on Giving Tuesday, you’re proving that in times of uncertainty, generosity can bring the whole world together.

With more than 10 million people in the U.S. unable to afford a pair of eyeglasses - a basic, human necessity - we rely on the generosity of individual donors like you, to bring clear vision to those greatly in need, especially during COVID-19.  The pandemic has caused many people who we serve, to lose their job, or their health insurance benefits. 

Through our Eye See the Future, Let’s See it Together campaign, our goal is to raise $2,500 to provide eyeglasses for people across the U.S., who are in desperate need.  

Help kids like Lily (pictured), one of our youngest eyeglass recipients, receive a pair of eyeglasses, and empower them to have a better remote learning or classroom experience, and quality of life. 

How can you help?

  • Order a “subscription to good” - become a New Eyes monthly donor! Your monthly donation of $5 goes a long way! 

  • Do the 2’s dance! Donate $2 to $23 to bring eyeglasses to the most vulnerable among us
