GivingTuesdayNow - May 5, A Global Response to COVID-19

May 5, 2020 New Eyes for the Needy

Join us today, on GivingTuesdayNow, a new day of global giving, created as an emergency response to the extraordinary need caused by coronavirus. 

New Eyes has been receiving applications daily for new eyeglasses from people who have lost jobs or health insurance due to COVID-19.

We have served the most vulnerable during the Great Depression, World War II and the Great Recession of 2008.  Please enable us to continue to serve during today’s crisis.

YOU will change lives with your gift by providing the clear vision necessary for adults to search for employment when the economy reopens. YOU will make a difference in the lives of children who need glasses for better home-schooling and remote education..

Donate online here or mail your donation to New Eyes at 549 Millburn Avenue, Short Hills, NJ 07078. 

GivingTuesdayNow is an opportunity for people around the world to stand together in unity—let’s come together to bring clear eyesight to individuals who have little to no other resources to obtain eyeglasses, in these very difficult times.
